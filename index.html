Introduction

Hello! My name is Shereen Elaidi, and I'm in U3 Honours Mathematics at McGill University. My research interests are ergodic theory, PDEs, mathematical physics, and physics (especially black holes). This summer I'm solving the wave equation \[ \Box u = - \frac{1}{S^2} \partial_\tau^2 u - 2 \frac{\dot{S}}{S^3} \partial_\tau u + \frac{1}{S^2} \Delta_\sigma u = 0 \] (where \( S \) depends on the Friedmann-Robertson-Walker metric) on various manifolds with random initial conditions. My personal interests include cycling, drawing, and reading.
